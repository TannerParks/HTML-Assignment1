Welcome to Hangman!

A children's game about execution

Tanner Parks

Hangman is a classic guess the word game that involves playing with the life of a stick figure about to be hanged. To be successful in this game, you'll need to have a vocabulary skills and not be prone to cracking under pressure. Their life is in your hands so don't mess up!
Rules:

  • Guess a letter from A-Z
  • If letter is in the word then those spots in the word will be revealed
  • If letter is not in the word then a body part is drawn
  • The guessed letter now goes to the graveyard so it doesn't get picked again
  • If the player guesses the word then they win
  • If all body parts are drawn before the player guesses then the player loses
